Our business is Virginia Beach Goldendoodles. A goldendoodle is a hybrid breed of dogs between a Standard Poodle and a Golden Retriever. They are very intelligent dogs, because the Poodle is number 2 in intelligence and the Golden Retriever is number 4. Goldendoodles were bred starting in 1986 to be therapy and service dogs, because of their love for people and intelligence. Goldendoodles are hypoallergenic, which means people with allergies can own them and they do not shed, which adds to their popularity. Goldendoodles come in many different colors. We breed red, gold, white, black, and sometimes two colors. They usually take their colors from the poodle. Our Goldendoodles average about 60 lbs.
