Thanks for visiting Virginia Beach Goldendoodles. We have been breeding Goldendoodles for 16 years. The health of our dogs is something we value above anything. We breed puppies that are well structured and intelligent. They make children’s hearts melt with happiness when they see them. My mother’s goal for the site is to educate people about goldendoodles and hopes the site will entice people to buy a goldendoodle from Virginia Beach Goldendoodles. Every time there is a new litter of puppies the site gets updated. We hope next time you’re looking for a new addition to your family, you will give us a call. You won’t regret bringing home one of our puppies.
